# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors. We expect:

- Respectful and professional communication
- Constructive feedback and collaboration
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at business@mail.sono.wtf. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
